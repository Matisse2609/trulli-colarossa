Welcome to Trulli Colarossa

An authentic trulli story

From the love for our land and the passion for hospitality, Trulli Colarossa was born. The property is made up of four authentic, independent mansions, fully equipped to satisfy every guest.

Just 1.5 km from the centre of Alberobello, the trulli sit in a completely private and quiet location, while still being close to the historic centre, supermarkets and restaurants.

Perfect for slow travel: wake up to birdsong, enjoy breakfast outdoors, spend the day exploring Puglia and return to your own peaceful trullo at night.

The complex & surroundings

Trulli Colarossa is an ancient, authentic trulli structure built in the late 1700s. Over time, the original peasants’ shelters have evolved into a small collection of dwellings, carefully restored with local stone and traditional techniques.

Today, the estate includes 15 trulli cones divided into four residences of different sizes and features, each completely independent. Outdoor areas are designed so every guest enjoys both privacy and plenty of space to relax.

  • Quiet countryside walks and cycling paths among trulli and orchards
  • Easy access to restaurants and local “masserie” for food & wine experiences
  • Ideal base for exploring Alberobello and the wider Puglia region
