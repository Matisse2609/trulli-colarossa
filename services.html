What we offer at Trulli Colarossa

A stay in a trullo means slow mornings, breakfast outdoors when the weather allows and time to enjoy the countryside. Below is a clear overview of the main services, facilities and experiences available during your stay.

Each trullo is independent and fully equipped, but all guests share the same access to the pool, jacuzzi, gardens and common areas around the estate. It’s a place for slow breakfasts, quiet afternoons by the water and evenings in your own private trullo.

Tour ideas

Wine, olive oil & masserie

We are happy to suggest experiences that bring you closer to the flavours and landscapes of Puglia. Below are some ideas we can help you organise during your stay.

Wine Museum Cantina Albea

The extensive collection of ancient agricultural tools includes pieces which would have been in use from pre-Christian times until relatively recently. Together with photographs and information boards, they guide visitors through the evolution of winemaking into the refined techniques used today.

Website: www.albeavini.com

Oil Mill Salamida

Oleificio Salamida, a family-run farm located among the trulli of Alberobello, produces high quality Extra Virgin Olive Oil, 100% Italian, using exclusively Apulian olives.

Masseria Caramanna

A long and narrow road leads to one of the most important rural complexes of the area. Announced by two superb columns, Masseria Caramanna sits in a majestic location and is introduced by an airy forecourt surrounded by countryside.
