Our trulli in Alberobello

Four authentic trulli, four different ways to stay

From the former farmer’s residence to the old stable and an intimate suite with private garden, each trullo has its own history, character and layout – all restored with care and natural materials.

Perfect for couples, families & friends:
Choose the trullo that fits your way of travelling. All guests share the same quiet countryside setting, pool & jacuzzi.

Below you will find a short overview of each trullo. Click on “View details” to see more photos, history, layout and supplies. Then contact us or book via Qualitravel.

For 2 guests

Trullo Gelsomino

35 m² · 1 bedroom · 1 bathroom

Once the farmer’s residence, Gelsomino preserves its original charm and details such as the oven used to bake traditional bread. Ideal for a couple, with bedroom, bathroom, living room with equipped kitchenette and private veranda.

Trullo Rosa

80 m² · 2 bedrooms + alcove

Once the stable of the complex and home to eight cattle, Rosa is now an 80 m² dwelling ideal for families or small groups. It offers two independent bedrooms, a living room with an extra alcove bed, fully equipped kitchen and private veranda.

Trullo Viola

50 m² · 1 bedroom + alcove

The tallest trullo of the property, originally used to store the products of the earth. After careful restoration, it now offers a double bedroom, living and dining area with two single beds in an alcove, fully equipped kitchen, bathroom and private veranda.
